About the art

Designing and crafting jewelry has been one of my favorite crafts for years. In my artisan studies, I combined my knowledge in weaving and jewelry to bring old traditional handicraft techniques to the present day. Recently, I have been particularly interested in the tablet weaving technique and delved into its possibilities.

In addition, I am very passionate about recycling and always try to use natural and ecological materials in my work. I offer a lifetime warranty on all my creations. That way, they do not end up burdening our planet in a landfill, and customers get to enjoy my designs for years on end.

Another significant interest of mine is utilizing technology to create wearable art. I have made one collection using a laser cutter, and in the future, I am interested in exploring how to combine 3D printers and even coding with my creativity.
