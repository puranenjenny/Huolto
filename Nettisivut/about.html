About the artist

Luna Maria was born in Finland, on the edge of a lush and beautiful forest. The forest fed Luna's imagination and she enjoyed building huts with fairies and other woodland creatures. Her grandmother was a seamstress and inspired Luna to learn about sewing, craftsmanship and art. Luna Maria was especially enthusiastic about sewing and made her own, as well as her plushies clothes since childhood. In addition to sewing, she is very interested in nearly every handicraft technique she encounters, as well as the visual arts. When faced with a new craft technique, Luna thirsts to learn the technique thoroughly.

There were a lot of grief and big storms in Luna's life and she noticed getting drawn back to her crafts for comfort. They gave her strength and peace to the anxious mind. Since then, Luna has worked as a craft instructor, as well as a special class assistant, until she finally got the courage to focus all her energy on her crafts. Luna Maria graduated as an artisan at the end of 2020, after focusing her studies on various weaving techniques. She is very interested in bringing old traditional handicraft techniques to the present day. She hopes to expand her skills for the rest of her life.

Luna Maria's biggest sources of inspiration are the Nordic folk tales, as well as the forces of nature.
